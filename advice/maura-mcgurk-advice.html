Hi my name is Maura and I have some advice for you!

Don't Go It Alone!

Find yourself a buddy - or even better - a whole group of badass Skillcrushers! It took me a few tries, but I found my people. We live all over the US, but meet on our own Slack channel every day, and regularly on Zoom in real time - to support, vent, and code! So reach out. Make yourself known. Find your badass partners in crime.

Go Outside Skillcrush Sometimes

As a newbie, I was nervous, but I started attending tech events near me: Meetups, WordCamp, happy hours...I might even go to the Lesbians Who Tech Summit in two months! I didn't think I had anything to offer these groups, but people seem to find my newbie status relatable, even charming. Everyone's been in these shoes, and people love to help. So wear that newbie heart on your sleeve and get out there!

If You're Going Through a Life Transition, Hang in There

It seems like many of us are; maybe that's what drew us here. We're doing the right thing by expanding our skills and becoming even more badass. Keep following this path you're on. If you're going through a divorce in particular, I see you. I heart you. I'm sending you good energy. It's difficult for sure, but we'll come out stronger on the other side. If you need to talk to someone, I'm here: maura (at) mauramcgurk.com
