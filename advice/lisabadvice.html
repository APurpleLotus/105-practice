Hi my name is Lisa and I have some advice for you!

Create a schedule!

The best way to stay on track with your Skillcrush classes is by blocking out a time that fits your schedule to work on your class. Stick to this schedule as best as you can!

When things get busy, it's easy to talk yourself out of working on your class, but if you have a schedule it's easier to stick to your goal of finishing!

Remember why you started!

When things get really challenging it can be hard to stick to it! Remember why you started! Is it for a new career? To learn skills for a promotion? To make more money? Keep these at the front of your brain to remind you to keep going!

Read some motivating books to build your confidence. I really liked Grit, Deep Work, and Atomic Habits. These helped me get in the right mindset for learning challenging things.
